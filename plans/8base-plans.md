# 8base Plans

Source: https://www.8base.com/pricing

- **Cost:** $0/month

- **Team Members:** 1
- **Database Rows:** 2,500
- **File Storage:** 0.5 GB
- **Serverless Computing:** 1 GB-Hours
- **Bandwidth:** 2 GB/month
- **Client App Users:** 5
- **GraphQL API Calls:** 100,000/month
- **GraphQL Subscriptions:** 100,000/month
- **Support:** Community only
